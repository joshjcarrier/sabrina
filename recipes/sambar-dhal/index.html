Dhal Sambar

Thick spicy lentil-based vegetable stew

********************

Sambar Paste

  1. Fry.
  2. Pound.

Dhal Sambar

  1. Boil dhal in a pot.
  2. While the dhal is boiling mix corriander powder, chilli powder and tumeric powder together with the pounded ingredients into a paste.
  3. Put two tbsp oil in a kuali.
  4. Fry mustard seeds, sambar paste and curry leaves on a low fire, continuously, till you get the fragrance.
  5. Dish it aside.
  6. When the dhal is almost cooked, put in the potatoes, cauliflower, carrot and capsaicin.
  7. When it is cooked, add in the fried sambar.

Sambar Paste

  • 1 tsp Jintan Puteh
  • 1 tsp Alba
  • 1 1/2 tsp black pepper

Dhal Sambar

  • Sambar Paste
  • 1/4 Kali Dhal
  • 3 medium size potatoes
  • 1/2 head cauliflower
  • capsaicin
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 tbsp corriander powder
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp tumeric powder
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
