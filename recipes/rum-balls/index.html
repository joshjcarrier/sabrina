Rum Balls

Chocolate and rum in a dense no-bake cake pop

********************

excellent!

Method

  1. Stir chocolate chips in heatproof bowl set over saucepan of hot, not simmering, water until partially melted (the water should not touch the bottom of the bowl).
  2. Remove bowl from saucepan and continue stirring until melted and smooth; let cool.
  3. Add sour cream, almond paste and salt; beat until well blended. Set aside.
  4. Put wafers, in batches, in food processor and process until finely crushed (you should have about 9 cups/2.25 L). Transfer to large bowl. Put pecans in food processor and process until very finely chopped; add to crushed wafers.
  5. Add icing sugar and cocoa powder; mix until well blended.
  6. Stir in butter and rum until well mixed.
  7. Using large rubber spatula or hands, mix in chocolate mixture until well blended.
  8. Cover tightly with plastic wrap and refridgerate overnight or until mixture is firm enough to shape.
  9. Shape 1 teaspoon (5 ml) mixture into a ball and coat with chocolate sprinkles.
  10. Repat with remaining mixture and chocolate sprinkles.
  11. Place rum balls on wax-paper-lined jelly roll pans; cover tightly with plastic wrap and place in refridgerator.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (500 ml) semi-sweet chocolate chips (about 2/3 lb/350 g)
  • 1 cup (250 ml) sour cream
  • 1/2 cup (125 ml) almond paste, crumbled
  • 1/2 tsp (2 ml) salt
  • 4 250 g boxes vanilla flavoured wafers (16 cups/4 L)
  • 2 cups (500 ml) pecans
  • 3 cups (750 ml) sifted icing sugar
  • 2/3 cup (150 ml) sifted unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 cups (375 ml) melted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups (375 ml) dark or white rum
  • 5 cups (1.25 L) chocolate sprinkles, about
