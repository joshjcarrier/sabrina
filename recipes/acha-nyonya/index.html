Acha Nyonya

Sour, spicy and sweet pickled vegetable appetizer

********************

Acha Nyonya

  1. Firstly, boil water with salt and a tablespoon vinegar (enough to cover vegetable). Add various vegetables once at a time (boil for a few seconds). Take out vegetables to dry in the sun or drain with a clean towel.
  2. Secondly, fry slice garlic and ground ingredients. Add in salt, sugar, vinegar and peanut.
  3. Finally, add vegetables, sesame seed and stir them well in the kuali.

Vegetables

  • Cabbage, Taiwanese
  • Long Bean
  • Carrot
  • Timbun

Pound Ingredients

  • Red Chili
  • Onion
  • Kunyit
  • Jintan Manis (optional)
  • Ketumbar Seed (optional)
  • Belachan (optional)
  • Serai (optional)

Acha Nyonya

  • Vegetables
  • Pound Ingredients
  • Oil
  • Peanut (fry, then pound)
  • Vinegar (according to taste)
  • Garlic (slice)
  • Sesame Seed (fry without oil)
  • Salt and Sugar to taste
