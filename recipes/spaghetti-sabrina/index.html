Sabrina's Spaghetti Sauce

Tomato-based with spicy beef

********************

excellent!

Method

  1. 1 tablespoon of olive oil in high heat and fry beef.
  2. Squeeze out meat from sausage tubes.
  3. Stir to prevent meat from clumping.
  4. Add in Ragu spaghetti sauce and the rest of ingredients.
  5. Add salt and sugar.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet extra lean beef
  • 5 Italian hot sausages
  • 2 796 ml sliced tomatoes
  • 1 369 ml can tomato puree
  • 1 packet sliced mushrooms
  • 4 cubes beef stock
  • 1 700 ml Ragu (spaghetti sauce)
  • Pinch of salt & sugar
  • 3/4 to full pot of water
  • Olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic - minced
