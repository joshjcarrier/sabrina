Aunty Sylvia's Sugee Cake

  1. Cream butter and sugar well.
  2. Add eggs one at a time beating really well in between each addition.
  3. Add vanilla essence. (I mix in the optional extras with the flour and semolina)
  4. Fold in all the dry ingredients alternately with the milk.
  5. Add brandy.
  6. Pour cake mixture into a baking tin which has been lined with greaseproof paper.
  7. Bake on the centre shelf of a moderate oven (180℃) for 1 hour or so until the centre of the cake is cooked. Test with metal or bamboo skewer.

Optional Extras

  • Raisins or sultanas
  • Coursely ground almonds
  • Citrus peel
  • Brandy
  • Nutmeg

Aunty Sylvia's Sugee Cake

  • Optional Extras
  • 1 lb butter
  • 1 lb sugar
  • 6 eggs
  • 50 ml vanilla essence
  • 1/2 lb self-rising flour
  • 1/2 lb semolina flour
  • 1 cup evaporated milk (consistency of the cake mixture - should be able to drop from spoon when tested)
